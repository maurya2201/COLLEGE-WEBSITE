1.MTech in Information Technology.

MTech Information Technology is a 2-year post-graduate degree program that aims to provide both theoretical and practical knowledge on the advanced fundamental study of computer-based information systems.The eligibility criteria for admission to this program is a BE/BTech degree in Information Technology or equivalent degree from a recognized university with a minimum 60% aggregate and a valid GATE score. The most common entrance exams are GATE, TANCET, AP PGECET, OJEE, etc.The average tuition fee charged by the most MTech IT colleges in India ranges between INR 5,000 - INR 3,00,000.MTech Information Technology graduates can be hired as a Software Developer, IT Specialist, Quality Analyst, Oracle PL/SQL Developer, Computer Programmer, PHP Developer, Technical Consultant, Network Engineer, etc. The average salary ranges between INR 3,00,000 - INR 20,00,000.

2.MTech in Data Science

M. Tech CSE Data Science & Analytics is a two-year post-graduate programme with an objective to impart knowledge on techniques and theories related to data science which includes statistics, data mining, data warehousing and data visualisation. The programme enables students to apply the knowledge of computing techniques and tools in the field of Big Data for solving real world problems encountered in various industries. This programme is useful for students who wish to develop and utilise their skills to achieve a thorough knowledge of data science and engineering. Some of these are the ability to aggregate interpret and manage a large amount of heterogeneous data resources independent of the hardware and software resources.

MTech Artificial Intelligence or MTech AI is a 2-year postgraduate course which is divided into four semesters. 5.MTech in Information Security and Cyber Forensics

This programme aims at equipping the graduates with advanced conceptual knowledge, technical skills, and the ability to pursue research in the disciplines of Information Security, Cyber Forensics, and Cyber Compliance laws. Learners are matured in specific areas of specialization like identification, handling, and mitigation of information security incidents through a meticulous set of advanced courses with a dissertation to showcase their research potential. The focus of the thesis is to solve an industry/society-centric problem.
