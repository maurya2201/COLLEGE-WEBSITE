SPORTS FACILITIES IN OUR CAMPUS

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Introduction:- Games and sports are not only important for success in studio but it is important for success in every walks of our life. college wishes that students perform the best in every field and hence provides its students with world class facilities for sports and games as well. The Institute encourages active participation of the students in sports especially through events like the General Championship among various sports teams through its internal tournaments; inter collegiate tournament and the prestigious annual Inter Sports Meet. Inter sports meet is zealously organized every year in which brilliant athletes from each participate to make their institute proud.

Objectives: To train the students to live healthy life, refine their sports and games skills and develop leadership qualities. To identify and nurture the sports talent through systematic PE programme. To provide excellent sports infrastructure.
